    What is the GuessGame Store?

    The GuessGame Store is a virtual store in which GuessGame players can buy Hints and Powerups
    with GuessCoin that they earned while playing.

    GuessCoin can only be earned by playing GuessGame. They cannot be purchased for real world money.

    Items available in the GuessGame Store:
    Hints;
    EXP Powerups;
    GuessCoin Powerups.

    How do I purchase items in the GuessGame Store?
    To purchase items in the GuessGame Store, you must have a local GuessGame profile with enough GuessCoin.
    GuessCoin can be earned by just playing the game.

    Powerups are only available in GuessGame 1.6 and newer.
