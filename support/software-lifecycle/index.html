    • Software life cycle

    RVH Productions software goes through testing before it is released.
    After the release date, software gets at least 5 years of support.
    This means 2.5 years of full feature updates and 2.5 years of maintenance such as bug fix updates and security fixes.
    Our software could last longer than 5 years, however.
